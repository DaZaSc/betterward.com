Retrospective Benchmarks

Evaluating contemporary AI against definitions written before the systems existed.

According to historical definitions of AGI, have we achieved it? The answer depends entirely on which definition you use. Scores range from 32% to 80%. This variation is not noise to be averaged away. It is the finding.

Chapter 1: The Gubrud Benchmark (1997) Brain-parity, general knowledge, industrial usability — 66% Chapter 2: The Reinvention Benchmark (2002) Single system, broad cognition, human-level competence — 80% Chapter 3: The Formalization Benchmark (2007) Goal-achievement across environments, learning, generality — 67% Chapter 4: The Corporatization Benchmark (2018) Autonomous systems, economically valuable work — 52% Chapter 5: The Critique Benchmark (2019) Skill-acquisition efficiency on novel tasks — 32% Chapter 6: The Synthesis Benchmark (2023) Levels of performance × generality × autonomy — Competent AGI
